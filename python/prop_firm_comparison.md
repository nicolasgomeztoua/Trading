### CONSISTENCY RULE CHECK (20% rule example)
If profit goal is $1,500 on a 25K account:
- Max single day profit allowed = $1,500 x 20% = $300
- If you make $400+ on any single day, you MUST accumulate more total profit so that day stays under 20%
- At $400/day best day, you need $2,000 total minimum ($400 / 0.20 = $2,000)
- At $600/day best day, you need $3,000 total minimum ($600 / 0.20 = $3,000)

**CRITICAL INSIGHT**: The 20% consistency rule means if your best day is $600, you need $3,000 TOTAL profit before you can withdraw, regardless of the stated profit goal. This effectively doubles the profit goal on a 25K account.

### 25% Consistency Rule (FFF S2F)
- At $600 best day: need $2,400 total ($600 / 0.25)
- More lenient than 20% firms
